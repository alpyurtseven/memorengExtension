Privacy Policy for Memoreng

Effective Date: 06.06.2024

Introduction

Welcome to Memoreng, an interactive language learning Chrome extension designed to help users practice and improve their English and Turkish vocabulary. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

When you use Memoreng, we may collect the following information:

  • Google Sheets Data:
    • Access URL: The URL of the Google Sheets document you provide.
    • API Key: The Google Sheets API key you provide to access the document.
    • Vocabulary Data: The English and Turkish words stored in your Google Sheets document.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • Google Sheets Data:
    • To fetch vocabulary data from your specified Google Sheets document for use in the extension.
    • To display and interact with the vocabulary data within the extension for educational purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data, including the Google Sheets URL, API key, and fetched vocabulary data, is stored locally on your browser and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Data Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data remains confidential and is only used for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Our extension may include links to third-party websites or services, such as the Google Cloud Console, for obtaining your API key. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Your Consent

By using Memoreng, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or within the extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
